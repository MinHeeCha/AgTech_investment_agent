# Sample data file: TechFarm AI Company Profile

## Company Overview

TechFarm AI is a precision agriculture technology company founded in 2021, based in San Francisco, California. The company is currently at Series A stage of funding.

## Mission

Democratizing precision agriculture with AI-powered tools that help farmers optimize crop yields while reducing resource usage.

## Technology

TechFarm AI has developed an advanced agricultural optimization platform that combines:
- Satellite and drone imagery analysis
- IoT sensor network integration
- Machine learning models for crop health prediction
- Real-time decision support systems for farmers

### Core Technology:
The platform uses multi-spectral imaging and advanced computer vision to provide real-time crop monitoring and pest detection capabilities.

### Patents:
- US Patent Application #63/456,789 - "Multi-spectral Crop Analysis Method"
- US Patent Application #63/456,790 - "Automated Pest Detection System"

Agricultural Benefits:
